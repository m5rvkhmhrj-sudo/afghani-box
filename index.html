Our Story

Authentic Afghan food, a whole new way

Afghani Box brings authentic Afghan cooking to Naperville with a simple idea: real, fresh flavors served in a box, made to share.

Everything starts with the basics done right. Rice steamed with carrots and raisins, kabobs grilled over charcoal, naan baked fresh, and homemade white sauce and chutney on the side. It is the food we grew up on, boxed up for the way you eat today.

✔
HFSAA Certified Halal Hand slaughtered by a Muslim, horizontal cut with tasmiya, and frequent inspections. A newly certified halal restaurant in Chicagoland.
